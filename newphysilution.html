What Can We Do for You

Spółka jest niezależną, innowacyjną firmą specjalizującą się w analizie farmaceutycznej i rozwoju narzędzi informatycznych i obliczeniowych dla farmacji. Wieloletnie doświadczenie założycieli Physiolution w dziedzinie symulacji fizjologii przewodu pokarmowego i biofarmaceutycznej ocenie doustnych postaci leku oraz zastosowaniu metod numerycznych w farmakokinetyce i farmakodynamice zdeterminowały profil firmy. Autorskie rozwiązania założycieli i pracowników firmy są podstawą unikalnych metod testowych i symulacji obliczeniowych podstawowych problemów powstających w procesie formulacji postaci leku. Urządzenia testowe i metody obliczeniowe stosowane przez Physiolution realistycznie symulują mechanistyczne i fizykochemiczne warunki pasażu żołądkowo-jelitowego postaci leku i umożliwiają realistyczną ocenę ich bezpieczeństwa i niezawodności biofarmaceutycznej. Możliwość zastosowania urządzeń i procedur testowych została skutecznie zweryfikowana na licznych przykładach klinicznych, a także w rutynowych analizach stałych doustnych postaci leku.

OUR WORK

What we've done for pharmacy

Design of bio-predictive dissolution tests in physiological design space

Our company is focused on biorelevant dissolution testing of solid oral dosage forms. In our studies we use Pharmacopeial apparatus (USP type 1 and 2) or innovative devices that are developed by Physiolution (AMP, PhysioCell). We investigate the behavior of solid oral dosage forms in biorelevant media under simulated fasted or fed conditions with bile salts and lecithins, representing gastric or intestinal environment (e.g. FaSSGF and FaSSIF). We also offer simple dissolution tests based on standard dissolution media. In most cases tests consist of two stages reflecting the passage through the GI tract , which is crucial for the evaluation of enteric-coated tablets or modified release formulations. Based on extensive experience in preclinical characterisation of drugs, we often propose to test formulations in hydrogen carbonate buffers - the most biorelevant medium to simulate intestinal conditions also in terms of solubility, so overestimation of dissolution rate can be avoided. The most advanced tests offered by Physiolution are carried out in novel dissolution testers, such as AMP or PhysioCell. These devices incorporate the GIT features critical for the dosage form dissolution performance, e.g. pressure, pH or motility. Our biopredictive power is supported by scientific design of experiments including Design of Experiments (DoE) and machine learning techniques.

Simulation of physiological and pathophysiological conditions of gastrointestinal transit

In our research, we pay a great attention to simulation of physiological and pathophysiological conditions including the composition and properties of the dissolution media. The composition, temperature and even the consistency might severely affect disintegration and dissolution performance. Besides commercially available and worldwide spread biorelevant media (FaSSGF, FaSSIF, FeSSIF), we often use more complex solutions, for example whole milk. We also pay attention to simulate the dynamic physiological and pathophysiological gastric and intraluminal pH fluctuations. Investigating the formation and stability of microenvironmental pH is an important step for the prediction of drug behavior in vivo. In Physiolution we developed a protocol using pH indicators and micro pH probes. The result of such studies is a valuable aid in rational drug development and prediction of performance in clinical trials. In the experiments we use our dissolution testers such as AMP and PhysioCell which make feasible not only GI pH gradient simulation but also an adequate and customized dynamics of the mechanical and physicochemical of in-vivo conditions. We take into account the specific requirements of the dosage form and we adjust our experimental setup and test conditions according to the gastrointestinal environment of healthy and diseased subjects.

Preclinical characterization of oral medicines and support of product development

Owing to the unique features of our expertise and the innovative dissolution testers, we effectively support the rational, physiology-oriented development of oral medicines. Our experts support the development cycle of oral medicines and help to improve the biopharmaceutical quality, reduce the risk of clinical trials and consequently reduce the cost of the development and shorten the time to market. We have also adjusted our laboratory equipment and developed protocols for solubility and pharmaceutical formulation biopredictive dissolution testing in small scale, i.e. approx. 20 mL of medium. The power of small scale allows not only API/formulation-saving testing but also predict performance of the formulation in clinical studies by performing tests in standard and biorelevant using different pH protocols such as constant pH, pH-step experiments or complex pH-multipoints programmes simulating full GI tract. Another important factor is related with disintegration, which is a crucial step that might severely affect dissolution performance of a drug product. Standard devices that are commonly used to evaluate disintegration often unrealistically estimate the disintegration time affecting the dosage form during the test. We believe that spontaneous disintegration is more relevant, so in our setup a dosage form is placed on a mesh and we let it disintegrate spontaneously, without reciprocating movement in the solution. We also apply a temperature gradient of medium, which might significantly change opening times for capsules. In the simulation of fed conditions, we introduce complex media of different consistency to more realistically predict disintegration in the stomach.

Formulation of biomolecules and analytical support

An important part of our work is related to the development and characterization of analytical methods. We offer support to our customers with development of analytical methods based on UV-VIS spectroscopy and High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) for the determination of small molecules and biomolecules such as peptides, especially in complex dissolution media such as milk and FaSSIF/FeSSIF. UV-VIS spectroscopy is performed using Agilent 8453 and Shimadzu UV-1900i. In our work we use Shimadzu HPLC systems such as Prominence 2030 Plus, Prominence 2050C and Nexera X-2 UHPLC equipped with Diode Array Detector (DAD), Refractive Index Detector (RID) and fluorescence detector (FD). Biomolecules, such as peptides, proteins or oligonucleotides, attract attention in modern medicine and pharmacy. However, their use is limited by the fact that they may lose their activity during manufacturing, formulation and storage. In our laboratory, we have developed a protocol including tests at different pH, with the presence of digestive enzymes for assessing the stability of peptides in the human gut, selection of the most robust molecules as drug candidates or prediction of oral administration efficacy for developed formulation.

Investigation of pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical equivalence

We offer full investigation of pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical equivalence evaluation. Starting from dissolution testing in pharmacopeial and more biorelevant conditions, through pharmacokinetic models and IV/IVC correlations; and ending up with comprehensive analysis of biological test results. We offer creating of individual pharmacokinetic profiles, including simulation of individual profiles using mathematical methods, interpretation of the results of pharmacokinetic studies and physiological mechanisms affecting the pharmacokinetic characteristics of oral drugs as well as cooperation in the rational planning of clinical trials.

PK modeling and simulations

As a company devoted to thorough dissolution testing, we strive to translate the in vitro results to in vivo predictions.We collaborate with acknowledged experts in the field of pharmacokinetics and modeling to take our highly specialized methods one step further to predict the formulation’s performance. Our modeling is based on the established semi-mechanistic models, thoroughly investigated in the scientific literature. We diligently investigate each case and take an individualized approach. Either for more standard immediate-release formulations, or modified-release systems prone to mechanical agitation - we put our best effort to interpret its behavior into the in vivo exposure. Our experts have experience with many challenging examples, and as a team we are ready to solve even the most demanding cases.

Development and manufacturing of bio-predictive dissolution testers

We are constantly designing and developing apparatus and equipment for dissolution testing. Our goal is to simulate intestinal pH gradients, mechanical stresses including the physiological intensity of GI motility forces. Our latest products are the AMP and PhysioCell. We also pay great attention to facilitating laboratory workflow. Our company unique experience is based on the unique hand in hand cooperation between lab practitioners and hardware and software engineers. As a result of this thriving cooperation we solve challenging problems in our and clients lab works by designing, modifying and manufacturing equipment tailored for researchers’ needs. Our laboratory team and engineers are ready to find a solution for your most challenging problems!

Programing for PK modeling and our bio-predictive dissolution testers

For more typical tests, we developed an in-house software that predicts the exposure in both fasted and fed conditions, and accounts for the variability of the human gastrointestinal tract. Our software developers create tailored and easy to use software for managing and automating laboratory tests. The team is also responsible for the software of our in-house testers.
